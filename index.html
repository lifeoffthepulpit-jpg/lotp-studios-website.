Our Mission

The church is more
than its pulpit.

Sermons get remembered. The doubt, the sacrifice, the clarity that shaped them rarely do.

LOTP Studios exists to record that part — honest conversations with pastors, ministers, and Christian creatives, held after the service ends. Call it Africa's growing faith archive, one sit-down at a time.

The Founder

Meet Eric Madaki.

Eric Madaki
Founder & Host — Life Off The Pulpit

Eric Madaki is a storyteller with one conviction: the church's most important stories happen off the stage. He built Life Off The Pulpit on that idea — sermons get recorded, but the struggle behind them rarely is.

What started as a podcast where pastors and Christian creatives could speak freely has grown into LOTP Studios — a media company producing conversations, games, and short films for the next generation of African believers.

"To build one of Africa's leading faith-based storytelling platforms — creating a lasting archive of conversations that serve the Church today and preserve its history for generations to come."
